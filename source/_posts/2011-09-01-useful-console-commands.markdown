Go to the end of the line
ctrl+e

Go to the beginning of the line
ctrl+a

Move by one letters at a time
ctrl+f (ctrl+b to go backwards)

Move by word
alt+f (alt+b to go backwards)

Delete words
alt+d
(remember that you need to be in the beginning of the word)
